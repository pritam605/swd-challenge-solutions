# Challenge: Normal as Noteworthy (June 2026)

## 📊 The Data Story
* **Focusing on the status quo:** Highlights how core European markets (UK and France) represent a major strategic victory of highly efficient stability.
* **Sticking to the plan:** Demonstrates that localized organic retention strategies are working perfectly without needing additional budget.
* **Clear call to action:** Recommends maintaining the status quo in Europe by freezing unnecessary paid media spend.

## 🎨 Design Choices for Clarity
* **Strategic positioning:** Framed the narrative around the bottom-left quadrant to showcase the "low spend, steady revenue" efficiency zone.
* **Contextual reference lines:** Added global benchmarks for average media spend ($71K) and average revenue ($52.70M) to visually anchor "normal."
* **Targeted color palette:** Utilized high-contrast blue tones specifically for France and the UK to draw the audience's eye directly to stable markets.

## 💬 Feedback Requested
* Does the bottom-left quadrant clearly communicate "healthy stability," or should a shaded background panel be added to label it as an "Efficiency Zone"?
* Is the large USA bubble too visually distracting despite being a separate growth market outlier?
